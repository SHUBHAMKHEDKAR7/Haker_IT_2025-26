About Sainath Electricals & Hardware

Established in 2002, we have been serving the electrical and hardware needs of our community for over 23 years with dedication and excellence.

Our Story

Founded in 2002, Sainath Electricals & Hardware has grown from a small local shop to a trusted name in electrical equipment and water pump solutions. With over 23 years of experience, we have served more than 50,000 satisfied customers.

Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for residential, commercial, and industrial electrical needs in Motinagar, Kolpewadi, and surrounding areas.
